Meet The
Professional Team

Dr. Patrick Liew Group Chairman of GEX Ventures, Dr Patrick Liew has been the founder and CEO of two different companies which he listed on the ASX and SGX. He was the former regional CEO of a strategic advisory firm and was involved in listing the firm on the NYSE.

Patrick has won numerous awards including the Global Leader Award, Asia Pacific Entrepreneurship Award, and the Entrepreneur of the Year Award for Social Contributions.

He is currently involved in helping companies transform their business models, globalise their businesses, and leverage the capital market.

Willy Lim

Partner at GEX Ventures, Willy Lim founded a technology company where he pioneered tools that allowed them to outpace regional competitors several times over to become the leading Mobile App Developer in Singapore & Malaysia.

Willy’s former clients include City Chain, Singapore Mint, Pet Lovers Centre, ASME, Civil Service Club Singapore and Rockwills.

He currently works with GEX Ventures to represent OTC Markets and the Canadian Securities Exchange in the Asia Pacific region.

Rex Fong

Group Chief Financial Officer and Partner at GEX Ventures and Chartered Accountant (Singapore), Rex Fong is an accounting veteran who has worked at one of the top 10 international accounting firms.

In an advisory capacity, Rex Fong has overseen multiple litigation support and forensic investigation engagements, and been consulted on commercial disputes. He has written Expert Reports and testified as an expert at the Singapore International Arbitration Centre, and the High Courts of Singapore and Malaysia.

At GEX Ventures, Rex Fong manages a business unit that empowers businesses to tap the global capital market.

Daniels Ng

Vice President at GEX Ventures, Daniels Ng is a consultant who specialises in catapulting SMEs to the next level with over 20 years of experience working with more than 80 Asia Pacific companies.

He was commissioned by the EDB to revitalise Singapore’s then flagging SME landscape, and headed the consultancy division at the Enterprise Promotion Centre. He was also appointed business advisor by the Canberra government at the Victorian Chamber of Commerce & Industry.

Daniels is a Certified Management Consultant with the Institute of Management Consultants (Singapore), where he is an adjunct lecturer in the Graduate Diploma programme.

Dr. Jong Hee Sen

Group Chief Executive Officer of GEX Ventures, Dr Jong Hee Sen has held top management positions in Education, Technology, and International Investment Management.

A pioneering member of the National University of Singapore’s Computer Science Department, Hee Sen joined the Government of Singapore Investment Corp (GIC) Real Estate Department, where he managed over SGD3 billion in investments.

Hee Sen has also been named among the Asia Top 100 CIO for his IT innovations. He was co-founder of two companies that he listed on the Singapore Stock Exchange, and was a member of the Singapore-China Investment Council.
